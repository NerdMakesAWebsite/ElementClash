Game Rules:

  • Each player starts with 20 health
  • Draw cards on your turn
  • Elements have strengths: Fire > Air > Earth > Water > Lightning > Ice > Light > Shadow > Time > Fire
  • Actions:
    • Attack - Deal damage equal to card power
    • Shield - Gain health equal to half card power
    • Heal - Gain health equal to card power
    • Teleport - Draw an extra card
    • Double Attack - Deal double damage
  • Matching an element against its weakness doubles the effect!
